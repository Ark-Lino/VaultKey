How it works

Three steps to secure 2FA

1

Import or add accounts

Paste a Google Authenticator migration link, or manually enter your Base32 secret key.

2

Codes generate automatically

VaultKey uses the Web Crypto API to generate HMAC-SHA1 TOTP codes every 30 seconds.

3

Copy or scan

Click to copy a code, or scan the QR code to export to another authenticator app.
